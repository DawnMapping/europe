Senior Money-Savers

🇪🇸 Tarjeta Dorada (Spain)

€6 card, buy at ANY Renfe station with passport. Ages 60+. Gives 25% off M–F, 40% off weekends on all Renfe trains.

Best buy: Barcelona Sants ticket office on arrival (before your San Sebastián + Madrid trains).

🇵🇹 CP Senior Discount (Portugal)

Ask at CP ticket window with passport. Ages 65+ get 50% off — better than any pass.

Best buy: At Lisbon Oriente for your Porto ticket.

❌ Skip the Eurail Pass

Money trap for this trip. Requires paid reservations on TGV/AVE (€10–25 each), doesn't cover Madrid-Lisbon flight. National senior cards save more.

⚠️ Never use Rail Europe / Omio / Trainline

These add €5–20 markup per ticket. Your $500 Montpellier-Barcelona is a €300+ markup — refund it, book SNCF direct.
